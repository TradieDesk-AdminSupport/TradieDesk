About TradieDesk

About Us

TradieDesk is a professional bookkeeping and administrative support service located in Sydney's Inner West. Founded by Jenny Nguyen, a Financial Controller for a $100+ million construction company. Having worked closely with hundreds of subcontractors across the construction industry, Jenny understands the challenges subcontractors face with invoicing, compliance paperwork and cash flow. TradieDesk was established to provide reliable and efficient support to help businesses stay organised, compliant, and focused on growth.

Our Mission

We believe that tradies deserve to focus on what they do best—their trade. We're here to handle all the administrative and bookkeeping tasks that take away from billable hours. Our mission is to free up your time, reduce your stress, and help you make better business decisions through accurate financial records and professional support.
